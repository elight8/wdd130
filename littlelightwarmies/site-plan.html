Instuctions about your LittleLight Warmie: How to warm up: Place your Littlelight warmie in the microwave for 3-4 minutes. You may remove the rice bag and warm it up separately and then place it in the outer cover. How to cool: Place your LittleLight Warmies in the freezer for 30 minutes to an hour for best results. How to wash: Remove the removeable rice bag from the outer cover. It is recommended to handwash the outercover with warm water and soap. Hang to dry. How to Purchase: Once a month we do a drop.
